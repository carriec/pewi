What is PE/WI?

PE/WI stands for People in Ecosystems/Watershed Integration. It is an online game-based educational tool that teaches complex interactions between humans and ecosystems through simulation of land use and outcomes in a fictitious Iowa watershed.

Playing with Land Use

Play, create, demolish and redesign
Imagine a watershed as a virtual sandbox

Time, Money & Energy

Real-world land use change takes investment
We need to understand the impacts of our decisions

Complex Decisions

Science-driven models aren't always user friendly
But they can be!

Our Sandbox

PE/WI is fun, simple & accurate
Learning about land use without investment or over-complexity

2

What does PE/WI teach?

PE/WI lets users learn about the impacts of agricultural land use. It's a risk-free way of playing with multiple land uses and asking What if I did this on my land?

Land-use Decisions

Human land-use decisions drive agricultural and environmental outcomes

It's easy to forget that land use is a human creation...

Land-use Outcomes

...and differences in land use can drive differences in agricultural and environmental outcomes

Agricultural Productivity

Production of annual and perennial crops and livestock

Biodiversity

Habitat for native species and game wildlife

Greenhouse Gases

Below-ground carbon sequestration

Soil

Soil erosion and control

Water

In-stream nitrates, phosphorus, and sediment delivery

Trade-offs among Multiple Objectives

May disappear in the long-term or be much smaller than you think

How to play

PE/WI is a web-based platform with easy-to-use interactive graphics. To play, evaluate all the factors, choose land covers, review results, and continue improvements.

The play is simple

Point, click, and drag

The results are interactive

Users can highlight results to display

Three years of play

Users can alter land use and compare outcomes over a three-year period

Sevel levels of annual precipitation

Users can respond to climate variability
